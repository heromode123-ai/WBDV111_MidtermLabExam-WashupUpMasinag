How It Works

At Wash Up Masinag, we make laundry simple, affordable, and convenient for everyone. Think of it like using a computer at an internet café, you pay for the use of the machine, not for how many clothes you wash. Our laundry service works the same way. You pay per load, not per piece of clothing, so you can wash a mix of shirts, jeans, towels, uniforms, and more in one load.

A single load can handle up to 7 kilos of laundry, making it a practical and budget-friendly option for students, workers, and families. Whether you have everyday clothes, bedsheets, or towels, our modern washing and drying machines are designed to clean your laundry efficiently while saving you time and effort.

We offer two types of services: Full Service and Self-Service. The difference is simple, it depends on who does the work.

For our Full Service (Drop-Off) option, you simply bring your pre-sorted laundry to us and our staff will take care of everything for you. We handle the washing, drying, and folding so you can relax or focus on your day. After a few hours, you can return to pick up your freshly cleaned, neatly folded, and properly packed clothes.

For our Self-Service (Do-It-Yourself) option, you personally use our washing and drying machines. You load and unload your laundry, transfer clothes to the dryer, and fold them afterward. If it's your first time, there's no need to worry, our friendly staff will gladly guide you on how to use the machines.

At Wash Up Masinag, our goal is to provide clean clothes, affordable prices, and friendly customer service in a welcoming environment.
