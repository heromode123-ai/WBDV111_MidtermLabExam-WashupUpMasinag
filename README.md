#### Short Project description:
This is our website that is 50%-60% completed about a Laundry business and its services in Masinag, Antipolo City. Wash Up Masinag is a reliable and convenient wash service dedicated to keeping your clothe clean, fresh, and well-maintained. It offers quality cleaning services with attention to detail, ensuring customer satisfaction every time.

#### Features Implemented:
The Features implemented in this include the navigation menu, responsive design, direct email to the business in contact section, showing of services, styled user interface, and device compatibility so majority of the devices can view it properly.
